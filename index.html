Who We Are

Strategic Intelligence Consultancy is a Kenya‑based advisory firm operating through a U.S.‑registered LLC structure. We specialize in helping firms navigate African markets with speed, clarity, and confidence.

Our work sits at the intersection of local market intelligence, regulatory understanding, and modern analytical workflows. We combine deep familiarity with Kenyan and African governance structures, sector‑specific expertise, and selective use of automation and AI‑assisted analysis to surface patterns, risks, and opportunities that are often missed in traditional market research.
