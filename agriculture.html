Why Agriculture in Kenya

Agriculture remains central to Kenya’s economy, employment, and export earnings. The sector spans smallholder systems, commercial farming, processing, logistics, and agritech platforms.

Success depends on understanding fragmented value chains, rural adoption dynamics, government policy, and informal market behavior.
